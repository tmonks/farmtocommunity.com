Our Story

It began in Lewis County, NY when one generous person decided to buy eggs from a farm that were going to otherwise go to waste, and donate them to the South Lewis school meal delivery program to get them to local families in need.

Supported by a generous community

Inspired to support our local farmers, prevent good food from going to waste, and get healthy foods to local families that need it, our wonderful community has jumped in and generously donated to our GoFundMe campaign to so we can procure excess farm foods to be added to the South Lewis Central School meal delivery program.

A passionate team

A team made up of school employees, farmers, and local agriculture experts has formed to find local surplus farm foods and get them delivered to local families. Some tech and business experts from across the country are also involved from COVID Accelerator to help put in place the needed technology and scale Farm to Community to more areas.
